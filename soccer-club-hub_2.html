📧 Newsletter — EmailJS

EmailJS sends emails directly from the browser — no backend needed. Free up to 200 emails/month. Connect your Outlook / Office 365 account as the email service.

📋 Setup steps (5 min) 1. Create a free account at emailjs.com
2. Go to Email Services → Add New Service → choose Outlook / Office 365
3. Sign in with your club's Outlook account and copy the Service ID
4. Go to Email Templates → Create template with variables: {{subject}} {{message}} {{club_name}}
5. Copy your Template ID and Public Key (Account → General) into the fields below
